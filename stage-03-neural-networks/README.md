# Stage 03 — Neural Networks

> Every AI engineer using models without understanding them
> is one bug away from being completely lost.
> Build one from scratch first. Then you'll never be confused again.

---

## Resources

**[Neural Networks: Zero to Hero — Andrej Karpathy](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VMj-3S1tku0&list=PLAqhIrjkxbuWI23v9cThsA9GvCAUhRvKZ)**
The most important resource on this entire roadmap. Karpathy builds backpropagation by hand, then builds GPT-2 from scratch. Watch every single video. Take your time with it.

**[Neural Networks — 3Blue1Brown](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=aircAruvnKk&list=PLZHQObOWTQDNU6R1_67000Dx_ZCJB-3pi)**
The visual companion to Karpathy's series. Explains what's happening geometrically inside the network. Watch this in parallel to build intuition alongside the code.
